About Us

Who We Are

Abhinaya Limited was founded with a mission to bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world applications. Our team brings decades of combined experience in HPC research, AI system optimization, product engineering, and cloud-native deployments.

We are a private limited technology consultancy company incorporated on 27 March 2023 and headquartered in Bristol, England. We are a micro-sized business operating in the information technology services sector, offering bespoke IT solutions and consultancy.

Our approach is grounded in technical excellence, collaboration with academia and industry leaders, and innovation from algorithm design to full-stack product delivery.
